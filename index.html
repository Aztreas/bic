The Event

The Berkeley Investment Conference is an annual national investment competition hosted at the UC Berkeley campus on March 16th-17th. The competition allows 40 passionate and talented undergraduate students from top universities across the nation to showcase their analytical talents, refine their investing skill-sets, expand their network and meet with people who share similar passions. Admission to the conference will be on the basis of the rigor of equity-analysis in a form of an investment memo, and selected teams will present their stock pitches in front of a judging panel of professional investors for a chance to present their pitches as finalists to win the competition.

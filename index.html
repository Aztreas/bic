The Event

__________________________________

The Berkeley Investment Conference is an annual, national investment competition hosted at UC Berkeley. Our competition provides passionate, talented undergraduates with an opportunity to showcase their analytical talents, to refine their investing skill-ses, and to expand their network of like-minded students and employers. Admission to the conference is based on the rigor of equity-analysis demonstrated in the team's submitted investment memo. In the first round, all participating teams will pitch their stock to a panel of professional investors. The top teams will then be chosen to participate in the final round, given the chance to win a cash prize.

What We Offer

Learn

Learn about investing from top-tier professionals and improve your investing capabilities

Share

Share your investment style and ideas with fellow undergraduates as well as employers

Compete

Compete against teams from across the nation for the chance to win a cash prize

When and Where

Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley, The 13th and 14th of April
